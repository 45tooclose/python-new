
2018 YC Investor School Notes: Andy BrombergEarly Stage Investing: Past, Present, and FutureThis presentation covers the past, present and future of early stage investing and will discuss (1) some trends that exist throughout the entire history of early stage investing, (2) the early days of venture (1940–1980), (3) the middle ages (1980–2000), (4) recent history (2000–2010), (5) the present (2010–2018), and (6) how things might unfold given the long term trends.It’s important to learn about the history of venture in order to understand what’s happening today. A lot has happened in this relatively new space, which is only about 80 years old.
