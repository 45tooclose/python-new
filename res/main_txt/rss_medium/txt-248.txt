
Distributed Ledger Tech for Public Good :)For the uninitiated, blockchain is a type of distributed ledger technology (DLT). Apart from the widely known bitcoin and ethereum, there are other types of DLT that can be public, private or consortium-based, permissioned vs. permissionless, and chained or DAG, such as Hashgraph.Over the past three months, our team has worked on two DLT PoCs with the Intellectual Property Office of Singapore (IPOS) and Ministry of Education (MOE). We tried out different types of DLTs and evaluated their capabilities in areas of performance, security, scalability, flexibility, ease of use and etc…
