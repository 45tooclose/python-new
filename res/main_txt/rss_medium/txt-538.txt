
New Tokens Added to BitfinexToday we are pleased to introduce trading for Aragon (ANT), Storj (STORJ), Dether (DTH) and Mithril (MITH).Deposits have been enabled as of 9.45 UTC, with trading for all tokens set to commence at 14.00 UTC.Bitfinex is excited to provide a platform of support for these unique projects, and to contribute to the long-term development of Ethereum. To learn more about today’s additions, please visit support.bitfinex.com.All tokens will be tradable against BTC, ETH and USD. Margin and P2P funding will be enabled for all markets as and when the order books develop sufficient liquidity.
