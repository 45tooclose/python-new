
Sucden Financial Posts £10.9m Net Profit for 2017, Revenues Up 5% The London-based prime of prime brokerage Sucden Financial has posted its annual results for 2017    Victor Golovtchenko  |  Brokers ( Retail FX )  |   Tuesday, 01/05/2018 | 10:20 GMT   Photo: ReutersShare this articleSucden Financial has posted its financial results for 2017, highlighting record revenues and profits for the company throughout last year. For the year that ended on the 31st of December, the company posted a net profit of £10.9 million, an increase of 30 percent when compared to the previous year.Net revenues totaled £48.7 million, a figure which is higher by about 5 percent when compared to 2016. Total assets of Sucden Financial grew over £11 million pounds during 2017 to £101.6 million.Commenting on the company’s results, the CEO of the Sucden Financial, Michael Overlander, said: “Our latest financial figures reflect the stability and strength of our business.
