
ADS Securities London Pivots to Retail, Posts a Profit for 2017 ADS Securities London has posted its annual results via a UK Companies House filing    Victor Golovtchenko  |  Brokers ( Retail FX )  |   Monday, 30/04/2018 | 07:46 GMT   Photo: ReutersShare this articleThe London-based subsidiary oil Abu Dhabi-headquartered brokerage company ADS Securities has posted its annual results for 2017. The company turned a net profit after posting a £3.1 billion operating loss in 2016.Last year the company posted a 36 percent gain in revenues to £5.4 billion, an increase of 36 percent when compared to the previous year. ADS Securities London also posted a profit of £461,000 with the administrative expenses of the company falling by £2.2 billion or a 31 percent cut.Total assets of ADS Securities increased by about £2.7 billion with cash and cash equivalents growing £2.9 billion as the share capital rose £3.5 billion between the 1st of January 2016 and the 31st of December 2017.Institutional and Retail RevenueThere is a notable shift in the product mix for ADS Securities London in 2017. The company has restructured its team and has committed more resources to the development of the retail portion of its business in London.During 2017 it managed to roughly double its revenues from the retail business to £2.1 billion (from £1.1 billion in 2016).
