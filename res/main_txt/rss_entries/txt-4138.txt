Thus, the forex broker was able to combine perks for its employees and an effective advertising campaign, as the cars were painted in company colors and marked with company logos.Branded AudisInterestingly, the provider of binary option trading, IQ Option, owned by Dmitry Zaretsky, undertook the same advertising move. Another Cypriot publication Cyprus Butterfly published a video showing a new fleet of 15 cars, made up of Audi A4s and A6s, carrying the IQ Option company logo.  As recently as December 2017, Demetra Kalogeru advised the Cypriot financial companies (CIF) to start preparing for new, complex realities.
