
PrimeXM’s Global Head of Account Management Leaves Company Lea Wang parts ways with the FX software solutions company after 4 years with the firm    David Kimberley  |  Executive Moves ( Executives )  |   Wednesday, 02/05/2018 | 08:01 GMT   Photo: BloombergShare this articlePrimeXM’s Global Head of Account Management informed Finance Magnates this Tuesday that she is leaving the firm. Since taking up her role with the FX bridge aggregation solutions provider in 2015, Lea Wang has worked predominantly in the company’s Cypriot and Chinese offices.Since starting her role, Wang has had an impressive track record. She lead a team that doubled client sales in a year and was responsible for establishing and training teams in 3 seperate countries.Wang joined the company in 2014, having just completed a master’s degree in international business. Within 9 months she was promoted to a new position as PrimeXM’s Global Head of Account Management.Alongside this role, Wang also worked as PrimeXM’s Head of Business Development for the APAC region. She was responsible for representing the company in media appearances and helped establish PrimeXM’s Chinese business through brand building and partnership promotions.Wang’s tenure at PrimeXM has coincided with a period of growth for the company. In May 2017 the company announced a total redesign of its website to reflect its growing clientele. The same month saw the company integrating MetaTrader 5 with its XCore system, allowing traders to access liquidity and risk management systems.Commenting on her departure, Wang told Finance Magnates that managing two roles had been “full of challenges but it had brought me much greater achievements and fun.” She added: “It was an unforgettable experience working at PrimeXM.
