
LMAX Exchange’s Sunny Singh Parting Ways with Company Sunny Singh is leaving LMAX Exchange after six years as its Senior Marketing Manager    Jeff Patterson  |  Executive Moves ( Executives )  |   Tuesday, 24/04/2018 | 13:21 GMT   Photo: Sunny SinghShare this articleLMAX Exchange is losing a core member of its team, with Sunny Singh, its Senior Marketing Manager parting ways with the group. Mr. Singh had been an integral component of LMAX’s marketing functions for over six years, and will be leaving to embark on a new business venture, Finance Magnates has learned.The departure ends a six-year stint for the marketing veteran who will now be focusing his efforts on launching a new business venture, which will kick off next month. At this time it is unclear who will directly succeed Mr. Singh, though a replacement is likely to be announced in the near-future.Mr. Singh commented on the departure exclusively to Finance Magnates, noting: “It has been a privilege to have worked alongside David and the team at LMAX Exchange for over six years. I look forward to my next role immensely, and wish the group the very best for the future.”Mr. Singh has been with LMAX Exchange since 2012, with the entirety of his career focusing on the field of marketing.
