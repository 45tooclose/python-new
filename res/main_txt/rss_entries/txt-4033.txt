

            The European Union’s chief Brexit negotiator Michel Barnier got a Brexit grilling from an unlikely source: elementary school children on his tour of Northern Ireland.“Do you have a difficult job, Mr Barnier?” the Frenchman tweeted ruefully, alongside photos of him posing with kids from Derry. “Yes, very.”His answer is the latest confirmation of just how difficult it is dealing trying to come up with a solution on how to avoid erecting a hard border with Ireland when the U.K. leaves the EU -- technically on March 2019.
