

            Saudi police said security forces “dealt with” a small civilian drone that was flying without authorization in an upscale neighborhood of Riyadh on Saturday.Authorities have started an investigation into the incident, according to a brief statement carried by the official Saudi Press Agency.The statement came after a video widely shared on social media in which heavy gunfire could be heard.
