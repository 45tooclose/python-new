

            President Donald Trump has deputized two top economic advisers to review whether to rejoin the Trans-Pacific Partnership trade accord he withdrew from after taking office, Senator Ben Sasse told reporters.Trump has designated economic adviser Larry Kudlow and U.S. Trade Representative Robert Lighthizer to lead the review, said Sasse, a Nebraska Republican.“That’s really good news for America and that’s really good news for the rule of law” in the Pacific region, Sasse said.
