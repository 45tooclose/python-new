
 Now you can pay for a sandwich you have bought on the way to work in Moscow with bitcoins.A shop gives this opportunity is Moscow ‘Subway’ – fast food restaurants Currently it has more than 39,000 branches in 102 countries all over the world. Moreover, it offers a 10 % discount to customers who take advantage of Bitcoin. Subway, most likely , the first fast-food shop anywhere in the world that accepts digital payments .First advertisement appeared in Twitter on November 3. Moscow ‘Subway’ shops are run as franchises. Most probably one of the local managers has made this decision to accept bitcoin payments and offer special discount to customers. So it is not a promotion of bitcoin given from the Subway company.
