
Space Quickly Running Out for Exhibitors at London Summit 18’ Catch a glimpse of the floor plan for one of the trading industry's biggest events of the year    Jeff Patterson  |  Brokers ( Retail FX )  |   Wednesday, 18/04/2018 | 12:35 GMT   Photo: ReutersShare this articleIt is only April still and the excitement for the 2018 London Summit is quickly growing. Hosted by Finance Magnates for its eighth successive year, the summit will take place on November 13-14 at Old Billingsgate in downtown London.Fresh off its largest installment ever back in 2017 that saw over 2,000 attendees, this year’s conference looks to be even bigger. The London Summit is a truly unique experience, helping bridge together individuals, executives, and thought leaders from around the world.The event will feature an emphasis on all segments within the trading industry, looking to network, learn, and discuss leading issues. This includes a packed slate of distinguished speakers, panel discussions, and hands-on workshops over the two-day event.Remaining space is at a premiumDon’t let the calendar fool you. With over six months to go the available space for exhibitors is already running low, owing to the growing popularity of the event itself. Each year’s summit is unique in its area of focus and the 2018 installment will be no exception. Don’t miss your opportunity to be a part of this year’s summit. Make sure to access the following link for exhibition and sponsorship opportunities.Regulations remain at the forefront of the 2018 London Summit, including a series of landmark changes in the retail brokerage and cryptocurrency space. Earlier this year, brokers have had to grapple with a combination of MiFID II, ESMA and FCA edicts, and bans for crypto advertising from Twitter, Google, and Facebook.Where the industry goes from here is anyone’s guess, though with all of the leading executives under one roof you won’t want to miss a thing. Additional topics will also include an emphasis on trading and technology in the forex, CFDs, and cryptocurrencies space.Be a part of the conversationThese topics, among many others will be on full display across a two-day event that is full of panels, workshops, and other seminars. Furthermore, the 2018 London Summit will also be kicked off by its annual Networking Blitz, constituting a gathering of the industry’s biggest players, executives, and CEOs in one locale.Prospective attendees are also encouraged to join the conversation and learn more about the event by exploring the event’s agenda in the coming months. The London Summit provides an opportunity to meet leading brokers, buy-side firms, ECNs and liquidity providers, banks, IBs, blockchain startups, and service providers.
