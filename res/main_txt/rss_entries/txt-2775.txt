
Monex Group Generates Record Revenues in March 2018 The Japanese broker’s revenues grow now for the fourth month in a row. ‎    Aziz Abdel-Qader  |  Brokers ( Retail FX )  |   Thursday, 26/04/2018 | 20:21 GMT   Photo: FMShare this articleJapanese financial services firm Monex Group has reported its monthly consolidated financial results for the month ending March 2018, which showed an upbeat performance on both yearly and monthly terms in what has been its best month in several years, according to figures released by the company.The Japanese broker’s revenues grow now for the fourth month in a row. Delving into March metrics, the group managed to secure a fresh rebound, reporting an operating revenue of $57.2 million (¥6.2 billion), up 37 percent month-over-month from $41.9 million (¥4.6 billion) in February 2018.Over a yearly timeframe, the latest figures showed a less upbeat picture, increasing by 32.0 percent year-over-year from $43.3 million (¥4.7 billion) back in March 2017.Financial expenses also registered an increase during the month ending March 2018, coming in at $3.8 million (¥412 million). This corresponded to a jump of 32 percent from $2.9 million (¥312 million) in February 2018. Year-over-year, Monex did see an increase of this figure by 6 percent when weighed against $3.6 million (¥389 million) in March 2017.Moving to Monex’s operating revenue after deducting financial expenses, the company reported the figure at $53.4 million (¥5.8 billion) in March 2018, a gain of 37 percent month-over-month from $39 million (¥4.26 billion) in February 2018.
