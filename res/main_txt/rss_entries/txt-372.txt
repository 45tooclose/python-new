
BitGrail Hack Victims Legal Representation Boosted by Nano Foundation Up to $1 million will be added to the legal effort to represent the victims of the attack    Victor Golovtchenko  |  News ( CryptoCurrency )  |   Monday, 09/04/2018 | 23:55 GMT   Photo: Source: Finance MagnatesShare this articleThe legal exchange between BitGrail and the Nano Foundation is continuing to escalate. Today the Nano Community has announced that it is setting up a legal fund for the victims of the BitGrail hack in February who lost close to $170 million worth of Nano tokens.After a class action lawsuit that is seeking a court-ordered hard fork of the cryptocurrency was filed yesterday, the Nano Foundation has issued a response to the challenge which is further escalating the legal fightings with the BitGrail exchange.Join our Telegram channel and get all the latest cryptocurrency news directly to your phone!The Nano Foundation elaborates in an official announcement that it has chosen to offer help to the victims of the BitGrail hack by sponsoring a legal fund. The aim of the effort is to ensure that the users who can not afford to be legally represented, are granted the opportunity to participate in a legal action against the Italian exchange.Meet Espen EngerEspen Enger, a representative of over 1400, supports the effort together Italian law firm Bonelli Erede. The Nano Foundations effort to support the legal fund represents a matching contribution to the one of the victims to the legal fund which was established by Mr. Enger .With the maximum contribution capped at $1 million, the aim of the effort is to support the legal case with a total of $2 million. For the time being the victims of the hack have raised over $300,000 in multiple currencies. The Nano Foundation’s move doubles the amount to over $600,000.Legal Action DetailsOver 1400 victims are currently looking for just in Italy with the Nano Foundation sharing with the community that those who are willing to join the effort have to send an email to Mr. Enger at bitgrailvictims@gmail.com.All of the persons will need to submit their name, country of residence and the amount of Nano in their BitGrail account. The lawyer on the case will be responding individually to every claim that is received.The Nano Foundation and the lawyer on the case Mr. Enger have agreed that the legal fund will be spent solely on the efforts of the victims to pursue their legal interests in connection with the BitGrail insolvency.“Mr. Enger has assured us that any money remaining after those efforts will be returned to the victims. Beyond the donations we make, Nano Foundation will not have any access to or control over the funds. We expect all future updates regarding the victims’ legal actions to come from Mr. Enger.
