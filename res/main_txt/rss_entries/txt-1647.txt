
ImpacTech Taps MetaQuotes Veteran Anthony Papaevagorou as Sales Director Mr. Papaevagorou brings 10 years of experience in financial markets‎.    Aziz Abdel-Qader  |  Executive Moves ( Executives )  |   Thursday, 19/04/2018 | 18:48 GMT   Photo: Aziz Abdel-QaderShare this articleFinTech solutions provider ImpacTech has appointed MetaQuotes veteran Anthony ‎Papaevagorou as its Sales Director, Finance Magnates has learned‎‏.‏ Anthony will serve ‎as a key addition to the startup’s senior leadership team to ‎spearhead its growth in the relevant markets, based out of the ‎group’s Limassol office in Cyprus.Mr. Papaevagorou will be responsible for contributing to ImpacTech’s overall ‎business development strategy and will play a key role in delivering the ‎company’s CRM solution to a wider audience. His experience and contacts in the sector will also allow him to help develop ImpacTech’s White Label brokerage solution. ‎Mr. Papaevagorou brings 10 years of experience in financial markets, with a broad ‎background in various capacities. Prior to joining ImpacTech, Anthony served ‎most recently as head of sales and operations at MetaQuotes ‎Cyprus. ‎In his latest capacity, he directed sales people and was ‎responsible for overseeing all activities and setting revenue targets for the ‎members of the sales team. ‎He originally joined MetaQuotes back in 2010 where he has built a ‎strong network of relationships throughout the industry working with several international companies in the brokerage sector.‎ Anthony vacated the position back in March, according to information made public on his LinkedIn ‎profile.‎During his eight-year tenure with the developer of most popular ‎platforms for retail FX trading, he hosted a large number of ‎MetaTrader 4 & MetaTrader 5 presentations, training sessions, various webinars and seminars.‎When asked about what attracted him to ImpacTech, he explained: “From the moment ‎ImpacTech collaborates with a client, we’re invested in the success of their business.”‎ImpacTech will be participating as Gold Sponsor at the iFX Expo 2018 in ‎Cyprus, May 22 – 24.‎ googletag.cmd.push(function() { googletag.display('inside_article_MPU'); }); Share this article Tags: executive moves / metaquotes / SalesRelated News Blockchain Hires Former Goldman Sachs Executive to Attract Institutions  OpenFin Welcomes Mark Yallop as Non-Executive Board Director  Dash Technologies Hires Jamie Bogen as Director of Execution Services  Got a news tip?
