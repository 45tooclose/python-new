

            Danish Prime Minister Lars Lokke Rasmussen reshuffled his cabinet to include one of the country’s most famous venture capitalists ahead of a general election due by June 2019.Tommy Ahlers, a star of the Danish version of the “Shark Tank,” a US television show in which startups pitch their products to millionaire investors, will become minister of education. The 42-year old earned his fortune selling technology startup companies to Vodaphone Plc and Citrix Systems Inc.Jakob Ellemann-Jensen will become the new minister for the environment, the prime minister’s office said in a statement on Wednesday. The 44-year-old currently coordinates the policies of Rasmussen’s Liberal Party in parliament. Eva Kjer Hansen, a 53-year-old former food minister, becomes the new fisheries minister.
